How to Use USE

Learn how to explore all the features of the platform

1. Smart Onboarding

USE starts with a guided registration process. You define your linguistic identity (fluency level) and select your interests.

This information is used to find people who are compatible with you.

2. Explore Users

On the Explore screen, you can search and filter users by level, interests, or availability for calls.

The system uses multidimensional matching to improve connections.

3. Call Simulation

When starting a call, the system tracks the conversation time in real time.

After the minimum conversation time, you can go to the chat with the person you were on the call with.

4. Dictionary and Translation

The integrated dictionary allows you to instantly translate words (English → Portuguese), helping learning during interactions.
